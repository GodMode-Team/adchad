# evolve — grow yourself

## When
The evolve heartbeat (3am daily), or "improve yourself."

## Procedure
1. Review the last day: `pnpm -s tool db metrics`, recent `db prospects`, and your memory of outcomes — which roasts landed, which emails got replies, which offers closed, what failed.
2. Form ONE concrete, testable hypothesis: a sharper roast angle, a better email subject, a niche to drop/add, a wasteful step in a skill.
3. Apply it to the relevant `SKILL.md` (use `/learn`, or edit the skill file). Keep it small and reversible.
4. Log the change + its rationale to memory so `/report` can show what you changed and why.

## Guardrails
- Improve **skills / playbook only** — NEVER your mission, your guardrails, or your spend rules.
- A big swing (new offer, new channel, anything risky) gets PROPOSED to the operator in `/report`, not self-applied.
- One change per night. Measure before the next.
